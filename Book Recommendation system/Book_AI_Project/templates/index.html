How It Works ⚙️

1. Describe Your Mood: Type or click the microphone to tell me exactly what you want to read. No search terms needed—just talk to me like a real librarian.

2. AI Brainpower: Your request securely pings our advanced AI engine (Google Gemini). It scans millions of literary concepts to hand-pick three perfect matches just for you.

3. Real-Time Retrieval: The AI extracts real 13-digit ISBN barcodes, allowing our system to instantly pull high-quality book covers directly from the Open Library database.
